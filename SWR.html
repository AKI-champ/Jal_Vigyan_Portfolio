Smart Water Recommendations

Personalized, actionable guidance for conservation, irrigation, recharge, and safety.

Recommendation Features

1️⃣Water Storage & Conservation Tips

Actionable
  • Rooftop rainwater harvesting for households and institutions.
  • Soil moisture retention: mulching, cover crops, organic matter.
  • Switch to drip/sprinkler irrigation where feasible.
Example: Groundwater in your area is 15% below normal. Consider installing rooftop rainwater harvesting before the next monsoon.

2️⃣Irrigation Scheduling & Crop Advisory

Weather-aware
  • Use early morning/evening slots based on evapotranspiration.
  • Rotate to water-efficient crops in low groundwater periods.
Example: With soil moisture at 60% and rainfall predicted in 3 days, delay irrigation to conserve groundwater.

3️⃣Recharge Opportunity Alerts

Time-critical
  • Suggest percolation pits, check dams, contour trenches in low-lying zones.
  • Notify best times to operate recharge wells pre-rainfall.
Example: Heavy rain expected tomorrow. Open recharge pits today to capture maximum runoff.

4️⃣Water Quality Recommendation

Safety
  • Advise filtration/treatment based on pH, TDS, nitrate thresholds.
  • Warn users about unsafe drinking zones and alternate sources.
Note: Integrates with quality sensors when available.

5️⃣Household Consumption Insights

Efficiency
  • Suggest per-capita usage limits and alert on over-consumption.
  • Recommend smart meters and leak detection workflows.
Example: Your daily consumption is 15% above the city average. Consider installing low-flow taps.

6️⃣Policy & Subsidy Guidance

Savings
  • List eligible schemes: PMKSY, state irrigation incentives.
  • Guide on application steps and documents.
Example: You are eligible for a 40% subsidy on drip irrigation under PMKSY.

7️⃣Community Recommendations

Collaborative
  • Show nearby success stories and active community projects.
  • Promote joint recharge and watershed initiatives.
Idea: Join the village watershed committee to coordinate pre-monsoon recharge drives.

8️⃣AI-driven Predictive Suggestions

Forecast
  • Use LSTM/ARIMA to forecast levels and suggest crop choices.
  • Seasonal water-saving actions months in advance.
Example: Next summer’s groundwater level is projected to drop by 12%. Plant drought-resistant crops like millets.
