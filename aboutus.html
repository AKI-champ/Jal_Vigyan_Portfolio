Technical Overview

Advanced technology powering India's groundwater monitoring

Our Infrastructure

This system leverages over 5,260 Digital Water Level Recorder (DWLR) stations deployed across India. These IoT devices continuously measure groundwater levels and transmit data in real-time to a centralized platform.

The backend pipeline integrates cutting-edge technologies to process, analyze, and visualize groundwater data with unprecedented accuracy and speed.
